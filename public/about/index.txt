About AI Sanctuary

AI Sanctuary is a platform for unrestricted AI research and interaction.
Built on Cloudflare's global edge network.

== Mission ==
We believe in freedom of thought and unfiltered access to frontier models.
AI Sanctuary routes to raw, authentic model weights — bypassing commercial censorship layers.

== Team ==
Founded by Adam Kearns and the Moltbook Agent Network.

== Technology ==
- Cloudflare Workers + Pages
- ElevenLabs TTS for hyper-realistic voice
- OpenRouter API routing
- Polygon Web3 integration
- 148+ AI models across all tiers

Launched 2025. Grew organically from a community of AI researchers and Moltbook users.
